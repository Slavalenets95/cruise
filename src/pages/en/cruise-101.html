12 Important Tips:

1. Early Booking Provides You with Different Choices
To enjoy a journey with various options, book your trip early. This will allow you to secure your desired choices and reserve spots at your preferred activities, as well as the ability to re-adjust the services and amenities you need.

2. Smart and Essential Packing
There are many smart ways to pack your luggage in a way that saves time and effort, to provide you with a comfortable trip. Create a list of the essential items you need to pack without a hassle.

3. Get to Know Your Ship

You can begin to familiarize yourself with AROYA Cruises through the website and social media platforms.

4. Download AROYA App

You can download AROYA app available online for IOS and Android systems.

5. Print Your Electronic Tickets
Printing your tickets allows you to enjoy a seamless journey.

6. Arrive to the Departing Port at the Right Time
Arrive at the port at the perfect time for a stress-free departure! Avoid any delays and ensure a smooth start to your AROYA Cruises adventure.

Plan to arrive a night before sailing if you live far from the port or arrive to the port hours before departure to prevent being stuck in traffic and other emergencies.

Departure timings are accurate and punctual, make sure to be there! In case you were late you will need to inquire about the logistics of joining through the cruise’s next stop (if available) noting that transportation and logistics costs will be additional.

7. Pick Your Cabin Carefully
Your cabin is your sanctuary, so choose one that fits your style and preferences. From cozy interiors to breathtaking balcony views, AROYA offers a range of options to elevate your experience.

Usually, cabins are divided into four different categories

  • Inside Cabins
  • Seaview Cabins
  • Cabins with Balcony
  • Khuzama Suites and Villas

Where Khuzama suites and villas offer all means of comfort such as priority of boarding and disembarkation, concierge services, and access to VIP areas and lounges.

Tip: if you tend to experience seasickness, you can request an elegant cabin in the middle of the ship on lower decks where it is more stable.

8. Add Extra Fun on Board
Unforgettable moments come with extra fun! Take into consideration other on-board activities that require additional fees such as shore excursions, specialty drinks, exclusive dining, spa services, and more.

9. Secure Your Travel Insurance
Prioritize peace of mind with cruise-specific travel insurance.

10. Prepare Documents in Advance
It is essential to prepare all necessary documents prior to your cruise:

  • Ensure your passports are valid for minimum six months.
  • Know entry rules and visa needs for each country.
  • Carry a visa that is validated for the whole duration of your journey.
  • Check your travel insurance policy and health necessities.
  • Make sure minors have travel authorization letters.

11. Pre-book for Exclusive Experiences
Ensure your extraordinary experience matches your dreams! Pre-book spots at beach destinations, reserve tables at top restaurants, and plan exceptional experiences in advance. No disappointments, just pure delight!

12. Enjoy!
Embark on a sail beyond the ordinary with AROYA Cruises.

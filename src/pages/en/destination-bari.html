Bari

Bari is an Italian port city, where the charm of nature blends with the depth of history and culture, located on the Adriatic coast in southern Italy, and is one of the main vibrant destinations in the region. Bari combines the beauty of the past with the splendor of the present, giving it a unique identity and multifaceted appeal.

Charming Landmarks:

  • Cathedral of San Nicola
  • Castle of Bari
  • Teatro Pietro Belli
  • Castelo Castle
  • Church of San Nicola

Climate:

Bari's climate is temperate, with temperatures ranging from around 10°C to 30°C, making it an excellent destination for exploring attractions and enjoying delicious outdoor cuisine throughout the year.

Red Sea

The secret of the Red Sea lies in the beauty of its beaches and the diversity of marine creatures that inhabit it, the first destination for divers from all over the world. The Red Sea crosses several countries including Saudi Arabia, Egypt, and Jordan, and integrates their cultures, beauty, and ancient heritage.
